Style Guide

My style guide mainly consists of images from manga and other websites similar to what I am trying to achieve with my website. The aim is to achieve a japanese manga aesthetic, with white and black beeing the dominating colours. I have found inspiration from popular manga websites such as Viz media in terms of creating a unique and visually appealing layout that my users can navigate with ease. My chosen font type is Arial, mainly because it is one of the most common and readable fonts present in manga websites. And lastly my colour pallettes, which i chose consist of dark blues and browns, but i will mostlikely go for dark blues such as navy or border closer to black and greys as I continue to progress with the development of my site.
